Welcome to the AOGIM Design System. This guide will help you get started quickly.

## What is this Design System?

It is a set of guidelines, components, and resources that ensure visual consistency across all AOGIM church materials — from the website and social media to worship projections and live streams.

## Who is it for?

- **Designers** — find colors, typography, and assets
- **Developers** — use components and tokens
- **Media team** — Holyrics and OBS templates
- **Communication** — brand guidelines

- **Tailwind CSS** — utility styling
