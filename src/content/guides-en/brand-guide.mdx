## Main Colors

| Color | Hexadecimal | Usage |
|-------|------------|-------|
| AOGIM Orange | `#d68020` | Titles, buttons, accent elements |
| Spiritual Purple | `#8b55ff` | Details, backgrounds, secondary |
| Light Background | `#f6f6f7` | Light mode backgrounds |
| Dark Background | `#0d0d12` | Dark mode backgrounds |

## Typography

- **Titles:** Inter Bold, sizes 2xl to 6xl
- **Body:** Inter Regular, base size (1rem) or lg (1.125rem)
- **Code:** JetBrains Mono
- **Hierarchy:** h1 → 5xl, h2 → 3xl, h3 → 2xl

## Logo

- Always use the SVG files available in Downloads
- Do not distort, rotate, or apply effects
- Maintain clear space equivalent to the height of the "A"
- Prefer dark background for the gradient logo

## Spacing

Always use multiples of 4px (0.25rem):

| Token | Pixels | Usage |
|-------|--------|-------|
| 4 | 16px | Card padding |
| 6 | 24px | Container padding |
| 8 | 32px | Section margin |
| 12 | 48px | Large sections |
