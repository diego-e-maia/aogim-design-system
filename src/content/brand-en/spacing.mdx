## Scale Values

| Token | Rem | Pixels | Usage |
|-------|-----|--------|-------|
| 1 | 0.25rem | 4px | Icons, thin borders |
| 2 | 0.5rem | 8px | Small inner padding |
| 3 | 0.75rem | 12px | Gap between elements |
| 4 | 1rem | 16px | Card padding, default gap |
| 5 | 1.25rem | 20px | Section margin |
| 6 | 1.5rem | 24px | Container padding |
| 8 | 2rem | 32px | Section margin |
| 12 | 3rem | 48px | Large sections |
| 16 | 4rem | 64px | Page padding |
| 24 | 6rem | 96px | Hero sections |
