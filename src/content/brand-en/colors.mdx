## Contrast and Accessibility

<Callout variant="info">
  All color combinations must maintain a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 for normal text and 3:1 for large text, per WCAG AA.
</Callout>

### Light and Dark Mode Usage

Neutral colors are designed to work in both themes. Pure white should never be used as a background in dark mode, and pure black should never be used in light mode.
