## Technical Guidelines

- **Minimum resolution:** 1920x1080px for screen use
- **Format:** JPEG quality 90% or PNG for images with transparency
- **Aspect ratio:** 16:9 for widescreen, 4:3 for print
- **Color profile:** sRGB
